Software requirement specification for  bus reseravtion system

Introduction:-
The website is to help the users to reserve their seat  in  the bus to travel for their desired location
Scope:-
⦁	They can book their seat
⦁	Can travel for their location
⦁	Can easily access the website


User Interface:-

The travellers can book their desired seat. 
And travel without any problems.
Refunds are available



Functional requirements:-
⦁	They should give their personal details such as name, phn no, etc
⦁	They should select their location from where to where.
⦁	They should apply for the date.
